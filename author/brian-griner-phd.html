Motivation¶

The Pima Indians in Arizona have been part of an ongoing study since 1965 by the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases due to the high incidence of diabetes. Many research studies have been done on this population over the years. A theme in this body of research is the ongoing debate around "nature vs. nuture" in determining the cause of diabetes onset and whether it can be prevented through behavioral changes.
